One month. Thirty days. Seven hundred and twenty hours. And somehow it still doesn't feel like enough time to have loved you.

I remember the exact moment I realized I was in trouble — the way you laughed at something that wasn't even that funny, and I found myself thinking, I want to hear that forever.

I'm not always good with words when you're right in front of me. You make me nervous in the best possible way. So I hope this letter says the things my voice forgets to.

This is only the first month. I intend to fill so many more with you.

Forever yours ♡ — always
